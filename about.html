Our story

Built by developers, for developers

Dev9 AI was founded with one obsession: eliminate the toil that slows great engineers down and let them do the work they actually love.

Our Mission

Giving developers their time back

The average developer spends over 40 % of their week on work that isn't writing code — triaging tickets, writing PR descriptions, chasing context, and sitting through status meetings. We built Dev9 AI to reclaim that time.

Our AI understands your codebase, your team's conventions, and your project history so that every suggestion, every automated action, and every generated artefact actually fits how you work — not a generic template.

What we stand for

Our values

Every feature we ship, every decision we make — these principles guide us.

⚡

Speed without shortcuts

We believe fast and high-quality aren't opposites. Automation should accelerate the right work, never cut corners on correctness or security.

🤝

Developer-first

Engineers are our primary customer. We measure success by whether we make their day better — not by vanity metrics or growth hacks.

🔐

Privacy by design

Your code is yours. We never train models on your private repositories and we give you full control over what data Dev9 AI can access.

🧩

Works with your stack

We integrate deeply into the tools you already use — GitHub, Jira, Linear, Slack, and more — instead of forcing you into a walled garden.

📖

Radical transparency

We publish our roadmap publicly, share our reasoning openly, and tell you exactly what the AI is doing and why — no black-box surprises.

🌱

Always improving

We ship updates every week, actively seek feedback, and treat every bug report as a gift. Continuous improvement isn't a slogan — it's how we operate.
